How does it work?

Have questions? Click here to email the maintainers.

Who made this?

This was based on Aftershock. Carson MacPherson-Krutsky at the Unversity of Montana had the idea to make it available for other locales and types of disasters. It was adapted by Melinda Minch and Eldan Goldenberg for that purpose.

Is this available for other locations?

You can make it available! If you can put up a webserver and you have the data, you can set this up. Visit us on Github for more information.
